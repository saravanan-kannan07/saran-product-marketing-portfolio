Product Marketing Case Study

Launching the Softura 7-Day Solution Roadmap

By Saravanan K — Digital Marketing Executive, Softura

Project Overview

Objective: Position and promote Softura’s 7-Day Solution Roadmap — a fast-track engagement helping enterprises achieve strategic clarity and align technology initiatives within one week.

My Role: Contributed to the product marketing strategy, competitor research, and messaging framework for the offering.

Challenge: Create a distinct communication strategy differentiating Softura’s roadmap service from large IT consulting firms while emphasizing speed, structure, and business value.

Goal: Build a clear, client-focused message that turns a technical service into an easy-to-understand, marketable product.

Competitor Research Insights

Analyzed roadmap offerings from TCS, Infosys, Accenture, and Cognizant to identify market patterns and positioning styles.

Key Observations

  • Competitors treat roadmaps as the first step in larger transformation journeys.
  • Tone emphasizes business outcomes over technology.
  • Companies use structured frameworks or branded tools (e.g., Accenture myNav®, TCS Enterprise Navigator™).
  • Most successful messaging highlights clarity, speed, and measurable ROI.

Application: Positioned Softura’s roadmap as a “7-Day Engagement” blending strategy and execution speed, a unique differentiator among competitors.

The 7-Day Solution Roadmap

Purpose: A one-week structured engagement to move clients from challenge to clarity.

Day
Focus Area
Objective
1
Client Consultation
Understand goals, pain points, and success metrics.
2
Technical & Business Discovery
Analyze IT landscape and dependencies.
3
Strategy Alignment
Map business goals to tailored solutions.
4
Visioning Workshop
Co-create the future-state architecture.
5
Transformation Roadmap Draft
Define initiatives, timelines, and ROI.
6
Validation
Align stakeholders and confirm priorities.
7
Executive Readout
Present final roadmap and draft SOW.

This structure helped Softura demonstrate thought leadership, reduce client hesitation, and accelerate project initiation.

Marketing Positioning & Messaging

Tagline: “Accelerate your transformation — achieve clarity in just 7 days.”

Value Proposition

  • Speed with Strategy: Deliver clarity and action within one week.
  • Confidence Before Investment: Identify ROI and risks upfront.
  • Business Alignment: Connect technology initiatives to measurable outcomes.

Messaging Pillars

  • Speed with Depth – Insight-driven results in one week.
  • Clarity to Action – From discovery to validated execution plan.
  • Confidence for Growth – Measurable ROI before large-scale rollout.

Marketing Collateral Created

  • Visual roadmap (PowerPoint)
  • Landing page copy draft
  • Campaign theme “From Insight to Action”

Deliverables & Outcomes

Deliverables Produced

  • Business-aligned roadmap document
  • Summary of current and future-state architecture
  • Prioritized initiatives with rollout plan
  • ROI and effort estimation model
  • Draft Statement of Work (SOW) for execution

Quantitative & Qualitative Outcomes

  • Improved clarity in Softura’s consulting positioning
  • Enhanced lead engagement through structured storytelling
  • Created a repeatable 7-day engagement framework for future clients

Marketing Impact

  • Simplified complex consulting services into a productized offer
  • Boosted team alignment between marketing and technical delivery

Key Learnings & Reflection
